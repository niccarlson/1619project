I am from chalk
 From Elmer’s glue and iPhones
 I am from the dog hair stuck on clothes
 (Tan, black, never goes away.)
 I am from yellow and red tulips,
 whose bright vivid colors always catch my eye.
 I’m from Chinese food and road-trips,
 From Jake and Tyler.
 I’m from the high-expectations and screaming
 From “do your homework” and “stop asking”
 I’m from fewer and fewer visits to church, no explanation why,
 I’m from Grand Rapids and misspelled last names,
 Chocolate milk and mac and cheese,
 From the visits to Pawleys Island every year,
 The empty seats next to me.
 In the closet in a bright blue box,
 Only a few family pictures
 and some pieces of chalk.
